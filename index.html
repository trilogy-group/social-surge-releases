Release 5.0.0

Date: 06 Sep, 2024

Say hello to Workers and Workflows - your new tools to create, collaborate, and excel seamlessly.

  • Workers: Empower Your Tasks with Precision!

    Designed to handle a plethora of tasks efficiently. Imagine them as expertly skilled mini-bots living in your software, eager to take on any task you throw their way.
    • Multi-Format Support: Whether it's crisp text, vivid images, or dynamic React components, Workers handle them all.
    • Feedback Integration: Each output isn't just delivered; it's crafted to perfection with your feedback.

  • Workflows: Streamline Complexity Like Never Before!

    Building on the robust foundation laid by Workers, Workflows is set to revolutionize the way you manage and execute your projects:
    • Chain Reaction: Seamless transitioning of output from one Worker as the input for the next.
    • Graphical Visualization: Get a bird's eye view of your workflows, understanding interconnections and dependencies clearly.
    • Context Management: Provide variable contexts to ensure continuity without losing the essence of the task.
    • Interactive Modifications: Provide feedback, and resume the workflow right from the last change.
    • Versioning System: You can effortlessly navigate through different versions of your outputs. Modify, review, or roll back to previous versions at any stage. This not only secures your work against accidental changes but also empowers you to experiment and optimize without hesitation.

Release 4.9.0

Date: 05 Sep, 2024

  • Long Video Analysis
    • The Channel analysis page now shows a warning on selecting videos longer than 50 mins.
    • The user can either choose to deselct them or continue with only transcripts of the videos.
    • If uploaded or auto-generated captions are available on youtube, those are used for the analysis.
    • Else we fallback to transcripting the video ourselves using cloud services.
    • In future releases, we plan to ananlyze transcripts for all videos by default.
  • Report generation now has access to all ingested videos' metadata along with the analyzed videos.
  • This allows us to show more relavant information in reports even if all videos of the channel are not analyzed.
  • To prevent the context from getting too long, we intelligently filter relavant videos whose metadata we want to include based on the report section prompt.
  • Static information has been removed from the 'Brainstorm New Viral Videos' report.
